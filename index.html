STEP 1: REGISTER

Use our online form to submit your vessel registry application .

REGISTER

STEP 2: INVOICE

We check and verify that your vessel and company has good compliance history. If so we will issue you an invoice. If not you will be notified of the reasons why your application was declined.

VIEW COMPLIANCE REPORTS

STEP 3: PAY

You will need to pay the vessel registry fees and then submit to us the proof of payment (i.e. Copy of the Telegraphic Transfer).

SCHEDULE OF FEES UPLOAD PROOF OF PAYMENT

STEP 4: ISSUE

We issue you a digital certificate of goodstanding. If your vessel is new we issue you a unique identifier for your vessel. This is known as your FFA Vessel ID or FFA VID for short. You will need to provide this to FFA member countries when applying for fishing permits / licenses.
